Ludwig van Beethoven

One of the most recognised and influential of all composers
A crucial figure in the transition between the Classical and Romantic eras in Classical music.

    Here's a time line of Ludwig van Beethoven

  • 1970 - the registry of his baptism, in a Catholic service at the Parish of St. Regius
  • 1779 - Beethoven began his studies with his most important teacher in Bonn, Christian Gottlob Neefe
  • 1801 - Beethoven composed his first six string quartets (Op. 18) between 1798 and 1800 (commissioned by, and dedicated to, Prince Lobkowitz). They were published in 1801.
